---
title: Templates and Examples
---

Use these patterns as starting points. Keep them short and adjust the output format to your needs.

## Summarize
**Purpose**: Turn long text into a short summary.

**Sample input**:
"Customer feedback: The new dashboard is confusing. The export button is hard to find. The charts are great."

**Sample output**:
"Summary: Customers like the charts but struggle with the dashboard layout and export button discoverability."

## Extract
**Purpose**: Pull specific facts from text.

**Sample input**:
"Feedback: The onboarding took 12 minutes. I tried it on Mac. The error said 'Missing token'."

**Sample output**:
"Time: 12 minutes. Platform: Mac. Error: Missing token."

## Rewrite
**Purpose**: Improve tone or clarity.

**Sample input**:
"Your request was denied because it was late."

**Sample output**:
"We could not approve the request because it was submitted after the deadline."

## Classify
**Purpose**: Label text by category.

**Sample input**:
"I cannot log in after the latest update."

**Sample output**:
"Category: Authentication issue"

## Draft
**Purpose**: Create a first draft from notes.

**Sample input**:
"Goal: announce Q2 launch. Audience: customers. Tone: optimistic."

**Sample output**:
"Subject: Q2 Launch Update\n\nWe are excited to share that our Q2 launch is on track..."
